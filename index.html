Principles

Skin in the Game as an Ethical Imperative
  • Do not get on a plane if the pilot is not onboard
  • Words are for sissies
  • You must be harmed if you make wrong predictions
  • If you see fraud and don't shout fraud you are a fraud
Worry about Fragility not Volatility
  • To succeed first you must survive
  • It's not all over until the fat lady sings
  • Systems without stressors are weak
  • Don't make predictions about the future, build robust systems instead
Avoid being a Turkey at all Costs
  • You cannot predict the future by looking at the past
  • We are blind to our blindness
  • Events that have never happened before happen all the time
